Welcome to The BEAT Kitchen Diner, where the flavours of nostalgia meet the groovy vibes of the 60s and 70s!
Nestled in the heart of Downtown St. John's, NL,

The BEAT is a culinary haven crafted by business partners and chefs Brenda Armstrong, Ethan Murphy, and Tina
Rowe.

Meet  The BEAT Culinary Team

Brenda Armstrong (The B)

With a passion for bold flavours and a knack for reinventing classic dishes, Brenda brings her culinary expertise to The BEAT Kitchen Diner. Her love for cooking began at an early age, inspired by her grandmother's Newfoundland recipes and a deep appreciation for comfort food with a twist.

Ethan Murphy (The E)

A culinary virtuoso with a flair for creativity, Ethan brings his innovative approach to every dish he creates. As the winner of Next Level Chef Junior, Ethan's culinary skills are already making waves, and his future in the industry looks incredibly promising. He is known as one of the best burger makers in Canada!

Tina Rowe (And The T)

A master of balance and precision, Tina's culinary skills are matched only by her unwavering dedication to quality and craftsmanship. With years of experience in both savoury and pastry kitchens, Tina brings a unique perspective to The BEAT, infusing each dish with her signature style and attention to detail.

Our Promise

When you dine at The BEAT Kitchen Diner, you can expect nothing less than exceptional food, friendly service, and a dining experience that leaves you craving more. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or settling in for a leisurely meal with friends and family, we invite you to join us on a culinary journey unlike any other. So come on in, take a seat, and let the good times roll at The BEAT!
