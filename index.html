About The BEAT

Good food and great vibes.

Welcome to The BEAT Kitchen Diner, where every meal is a symphony of flavor! Located at 12345 Water Street West in the heart of St. John's, NL, our retro-inspired diner invites you to step back in time to the groovy vibes of the 60s and 70s. From our funky appetizers to our soulful entrees and refreshing drinks, we serve up a menu that's as vibrant as the era it's inspired by. Whether you're craving a classic burger with a twist or looking to sip on a retro cocktail, our friendly staff is here to make your dining experience unforgettable.
